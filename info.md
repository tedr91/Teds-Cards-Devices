## Ted's Device Cards

A collection of custom Lovelace cards for **specific devices**, sharing the look and feel of [Ted's Cards](https://github.com/tedr91/HA-Teds-Cards).

| Card | Type | Device integration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ted AV Receiver Card | `custom:ted-av-receiver-card` | [HA-DenonMarantz](https://github.com/tedr91/HA-DenonMarantz) |
| Ted NovaStar H Card | `custom:ted-novastar-card` | [HA-novastar-h](https://github.com/tedr91/HA-novastar-h) |

Each card includes a visual editor. See the [README](https://github.com/tedr91/Teds-Cards-Devices#readme) for full configuration options.
